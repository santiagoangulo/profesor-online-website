# General Mathematics (Matemáticas Generales) — Content Index

**Subject:** Matemáticas Generales (General Mathematics)  
**Level:** 1st Year Bachillerato — General Modality (*único curso*; this subject has no continuation in 2nd year Bachillerato)  
**Curriculum framework:** Decreto 64/2022 (Comunidad de Madrid), LOMLOE  

## Note on the nature of this subject

General Mathematics is an applied, context-driven subject. Its purpose is to equip students with mathematical tools relevant to everyday, financial, social, and technological situations. The official curriculum organises content into five "mathematical senses" (*sentidos*); this index reorganises those into **12 didactic chapters** to facilitate instructional sequencing and exercise assignment. Every third-level subsection includes, in brackets, the official curriculum block (*bloque de saberes básicos*) it maps to.
